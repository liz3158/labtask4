Today Is a Good Day to Donate to a Children’s Charity You Can Trust

Right now, the world's children are growing up in a deadly pandemic – their lives and futures on the line. But through the dedicated support of donors like you, Save the Children is on the front lines, doing whatever it takes to protect children in the U.S. and around the world.

Your charity donation helps create positive change that children need right now.




Thanks to your charity donations, we've achieved tremendous results for children.

  • Since our founding over 100 years ago, we’ve changed the lives of over 1 billion children.
  • Since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, globally, we’ve trained or supported 95,000 health workers and provided 762,000 families with access to safe water.
  • We’ve supported distance learning for 2.9 million children across the world, including 90,000 children with disabilities.
  • Across America, we’ve delivered nearly 9 million nourishing meals to children in poor, rural communities.

All this means one thing — your charity donation helps change children’s lives




What Makes Save the Children a Children’s Charity You Can Trust?

Save the Children is devoted solely to serving children’s needs and securing their rights. This lifesaving work has been recognized by charity watch groups.

Recently, we were proud to receive our 16th four-star rating from Charity Navigator for sound fiscal management and a proven commitment to accountability and transparency.

You can rest assured that your charity donation to Save the Children is one of the world’s best investments. In fact, we keep administrative costs low so that more funding goes to children’s programs.

86% of every dollar you give goes to programs and services for children and families in need.
